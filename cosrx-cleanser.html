Key Benefits & Skin Type

  • Exfoliates inside pores with 0.5% salicylic acid (BHA) to help clear blackheads and reduce breakouts
  • Mild, non-drying foam that cleanses without stripping the skin barrier
  • Soothed with tea tree leaf oil and willow bark water to calm redness and irritation
  • Free from harsh sulfates, parabens, and silicones

Best for: Best for oily, combination, and acne-prone skin. Gentle enough for daily use on sensitive skin, though those with very dry skin may prefer to use it every other day.

How to Use

Dispense a small amount into your palm and lather with warm water. Massage gently onto damp skin in circular motions, avoiding the eye area, focusing on the T-zone where oil builds up most. Rinse thoroughly with lukewarm water. Use once or twice daily, morning and/or evening.
